Frequently Asked Questions

What type of server performance can I expect?

Our servers are powered by the latest AMD EPYC 9754 processors, ensuring exceptional performance for your Minecraft server. Combined with NVMe storage and high-frequency DDR4 RAM, you can expect minimal lag and smooth gameplay even with multiple players and mods.

How do I upgrade my plan if I need more resources?

Upgrading your plan is simple! Just log into your control panel, navigate to the "Billing" section, and select "Upgrade Plan." Your server will be automatically migrated to the new plan with minimal downtime, and you'll only be charged the difference in price for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

Absolutely not! We believe in transparent pricing. The price you see is the price you pay - there are no setup fees, hidden costs, or surprise charges. All our plans include 24/7 support, DDoS protection, and our user-friendly control panel at no extra cost.

What support do you offer if I have problems?

We provide 24/7 support through our ticket system with an average response time of under 30 minutes. Our support team consists of Minecraft experts who can help with server configuration, plugin issues, and general troubleshooting. Premium and higher plans also include priority support with even faster response times.

Can I install custom mods and plugins?

Yes! All our plans support custom mods, plugins, and modpacks. Our control panel makes it easy to install popular modpacks with just one click, and you have full FTP access to upload and manage your custom content. If you need help setting up specific mods, our support team is always ready to assist.
